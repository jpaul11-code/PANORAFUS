# Contribute to PANORAFUS.AI

Thank you for helping PANORAFUS.AI expand its global directory of institutions, ministries, and faith-based networks.

---

## Who Can Contribute

- Religious institutions and denominations
- Humanitarian and service organizations
- Seminaries, schools, and faith-based education bodies
- Prayer, missions, media, and interfaith networks
- Individual contributors with verifiable institutional information

---

## What You Can Submit

You may submit:

- New institutions to be added
- Corrections to institution names, locations, or descriptions
- Updated official websites or contact channels
- New category suggestions for future PANORAFUS.AI indexes

---

## Required Information for New Entries

Please include:

1. **Institution name**
2. **Country and city**
3. **Religious tradition / category**
4. **Short description** (one sentence)
5. **Official website URL**
6. **Why this institution should be included in PANORAFUS.AI**

---

## Submission Standards

All submissions should be:

- Accurate and verifiable
- Respectful across traditions and denominations
- Focused on organizations with public mission impact
- Free from hate speech, defamation, and misinformation

PANORAFUS.AI reserves the right to review and curate submissions for quality, clarity, and alignment with repository scope.

## Review Process

1. Submission received
2. Basic verification of name, location, and mission
3. Category placement into the most relevant PANORAFUS.AI index
4. Publication in the next documentation update cycle
